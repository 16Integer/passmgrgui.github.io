Commonly Asked Questions

1. How can I reset my master password?

Warning: This WILL erase all your password data for security purposes.
To reset your master password, simply remove the masterpassword file in your specified OS's directory. Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%/
Linux: ~\.config\
MacOS: ~\Libary\Application Support\
